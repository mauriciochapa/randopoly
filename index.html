Why We Love Randopoly

Questions

  • Why is Randopoly different?

    • To begin with, Randopoly is a game, and it’s random, so it’s fun. We are actually very different and a 1-of-a-kind service! Randopoly offers the ability to get free samples/coupons/products from many companies they may never have heard about AND the brands they already love. Sometimes you get a coupon, sometimes a free sample, and sometimes a full version of a product, absolutely free.

  • What can I win?

    • You can win any one or combination of the following per play: i. Samples – These are typically smaller versions of the product; think travel-sized bottles and the like. Samples are a physical product you will receive by post. ii. Coupons – Not your typical “Save $1 off your next purchase of $100” coupons. These will be coupons you WANT to cash in on, and will offer substantial discounts, etc. They will be e-mailed to you, so no need to wait around for the mailman! iii. Products – We will regularly have full versions of products available, and like all other prizes, they will be offered in a completely random fashion. Would you expect anything else from Randopoly?

  • Is my contact information safe?

    • 100% safe! Your details will only be used by Randopoly and the company that will be providing you with your free sample/coupon/product. You will be able to see which company you will be agreeing to receive a prize from before actually accepting that prize.

  • What companies do you work with?

    • We currently work with Canadian companies that specialize in various types of cosmetics/make-up, skincare and personal care products. Our list will continue to grow and we aim to form partnerships with small mom-and-pop local producers, as well as large industry leaders and everything in between.

  • Why would a company want to work with Randopoly?

    • One of the most important objectives of almost any company is expand its customer base. The companies we work with are getting new potential customers to send their samples/coupons/products to. If the user happens to love their product, the company now has a new customer that they may not otherwise have found.

  • Who can play Randopoly?

    • Anyone that is at least 18 years old and lives in either Canada or the United States can play Randopoly.
